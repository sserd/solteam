Who are we ?


An interesting group of people from a broad academic background who have come together for once to have fun working on Helio-Physics. While a research work isn't just another easy task to carry out, it is for the same reason that having a joyous time doing it is as important. Well, this is a team of eleven and though it brought with it a bunch of technical skills, it brought with it a bunch of ideas to relax and have fun too, which turned out to be one significant part of the internship part from the most important one - the research work itself. Ultimately, the team together succeeded in getting the work done, predicting efficiently the occurrence of solar flares using Machine Leaning.
